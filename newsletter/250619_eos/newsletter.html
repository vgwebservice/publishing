Burning Ignea is almost here, and it's packed with opportunities for all kinds of players. Whether you're chasing DPS rankings, mob farming, or treasure hunting, this update is built to reward your effort and playstyle.

What is Burning Ignea?

Burning Ignea is a time-limited field area you can enter once per day for 1 hour. Inside, you'll find 4 world bosses, elite mobs, and valuable treasure chests scattered across the map. Choose your strategy — fight, farm, or loot — and make every minute count.

What are the Rewards?

1) Token of Alliance
Earn this new currency by:
· Defeating Mobs
· Damaging Bosses (1 Damage+)
· Ranking in the Top 10 DPS for Bosses


2) Exchange Tokens at the event NPC for:
· Inherited Asgardian Ore / Asgardian Ore
· Inherited Conqueror/Einherjar Essences
· High-Grade Enhancement Materials


3) Treasure Chests
Some chests can be looted without fighting. The Locked Ignea Treasure Chest can drop:
· Special Lucky Treasure Box
· Transcended Equipment Boxes
· Rare Costume Boxes
· Pegasus Mounts
· Premium Consumables
Burning Ignea supports all playstyles. Whether you're a top-tier damage dealer, a solo grinder, or a clever looter, this update lets you earn rewards your way.

Prepare for June 19th (CET). Plan your route, rally your team, and get ready to dive into the fire!
