Located in Sagres, this new established school exclusively for Aides opens its doors to the finest, offering them opportunities to enhance their skills and knowledge.
Join one of the elites and become a part of the future of nautical excellence!
👉 The Rise of Bandit Chieftains in Land Battles
Once players have defeated a certain number of enemies in the same Land, a special enemy known as the 'Bandit Chieftain' will emerge to reclaim dominion over the Land.
Although highly dangerous, defeating them promises valuable rewards!

Three new buildable ships - Neva, Keying and Great Micheal are awaiting you!
