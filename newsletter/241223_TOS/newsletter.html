Merge Incoming on January 7th, 2025!
We're excited to announce that the Popolion and Kepa servers will be merging into a single server named "Kepa".

Teams will be auto-transferred based on our Server Merge Policy. Want to choose which team to keep? Submit the pre-registration form now!


Why Merge Now?
Many players have eagerly awaited this merge, and the timing is finally right. Kepa's teams have reached a mature level of progression, with their top players having higher average character levels and silver compared to Popolion. This merge also addresses a reduced population across both servers, ensuring a more vibrant and enjoyable gaming experience.
